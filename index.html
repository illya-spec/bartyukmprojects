About us

Founded in 1989, BartyukmProjects has been a pioneer in the world of arcade gaming for over three decades. We specialize in the design, manufacturing, and sale of high-quality arcade machines that bring classic gaming experiences to life with a modern twist. Our mission has always been clear: to preserve the magic of arcade entertainment while pushing the boundaries of innovation and quality. Unlike many in the industry, BartyukmProjects stands firmly against mass-produced, low-quality machines flooding the market—particularly from companies like FutureGame, who prioritize quantity over the authentic arcade spirit. We take pride in our craftsmanship, our attention to detail, and our unwavering commitment to providing arcade enthusiasts with machines that are built to last and designed to impress. At BartyukmProjects, we don't just sell arcade machines—we deliver nostalgia, excitement, and the timeless joy of gaming. Join us and experience the difference that passion and tradition make.
